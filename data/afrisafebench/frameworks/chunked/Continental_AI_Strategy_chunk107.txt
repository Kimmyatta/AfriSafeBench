Associated Actions • Support AU Member States’ efforts to formulate and implement national policies on AI in education • Support AU Member States to develop validation mechanisms to verify AI systems to be adopted in education • Develop AI competency frameworks and appropriate training programmes in alignment with digital and/or general competency frameworks for teachers. • Develop AI competency frameworks for students and enhance government- sanctioned curriculum or education programmes on AI. • Assess skills requirements at basic, secondary and tertiary education levels to identify key challenges that Member States face in building AI skills. • Support AU Member States to develop national AI competencies for teachers and students. • Designate centres of excellence in Africa to conduct analysis on AI in education and build a knowledge base of policies, frameworks, indigenous educational AI tools, and use cases in the continent.
