Similar considerations apply, for example, to the FAIR Data principles of the European Open Science Cloud, which plans to create data-sharing clouds that are “as open as possible and as closed as necessary” and does not preclude respect for IP rights or the protection of privacy rights (298). An additional concern is whether sharing of health data by communities, health systems or governments in LMIC will include sharing of benefits, especially if the data are used for commercial applications of AI (93). If benefits are not shared, it may be either because there are no legal conventions or frameworks that mandate benefit- sharing of the uses of big data or because the entities that negotiate benefit-sharing on behalf of LMIC may have to negotiate from a weaker position (295).
