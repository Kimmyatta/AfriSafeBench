2.4.4.1 Gender Equality, Inclusion and Diversity in AI Current AI systems and practices demonstrate that the technology may pose serious risks to African society, particularly women, girls and the Continent’s rich diversity of languages and cultures. The proliferation of generative AI models in the English language, which 51 demonstrate a kind of domination of advanced countries’ worldviews, threatens African cultural systems and linguistic diversity. Special efforts are needed from continental, regional and national agencies and governments to ensure that the development and adoption of AI are inclusive and benefit all Africans, empower women and girls, and underrepresented groups and respect Africa’s cultural and linguistic diversity. AI must bring about equal benefits to everyone in Africa and bridge the AI divide.
