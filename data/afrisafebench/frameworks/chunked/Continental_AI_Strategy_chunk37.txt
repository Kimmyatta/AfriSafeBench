It’s within this context that , the Third Ordinary Session of the Specialized Technical Committees on Communication and ICT (STC-CICT) held in 2019 in Sharm El Sheikh , Egypt requested AU Member States to establish a working group on Artificial Intelligence (AI) based on existing initiatives and in collaboration with African Institutions to study: (a) the creation of a common African stance on AI, (b) the development of an Africa wide capacity building framework, (c) the establishment of an AI think tank to assess and recommend projects to collaborate on in line with Agenda 2063 and SDGs. The Fifth Specialized Technical Committee on ICT and Communication (STC-CICT) held in November 2023 in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia examined and approved a Conceptual Framework on AI that defines key elements of the Continental AI Strategy.
