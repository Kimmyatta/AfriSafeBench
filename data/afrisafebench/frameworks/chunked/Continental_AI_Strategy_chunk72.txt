Mission The Mission of the AI Strategy is to harness AI for accelerating social and economic transformation and promoting cultural renaissance in Africa in line with the AU Agenda 2063 and the Sustainable Development Goals, to minimise the risks that AI poses to African people and countries, and to accelerate the development of the necessary AI capabilities of the AU Member States. 2.2 Guiding Principles The following high-level principles guide the AI Strategy: 27 Local First - The production, development, use and assessment of AI in Africa will be foremost to address African challenges like healthcare delivery, food security, clean energy, climate change and water management and opportunities with African solutions. The growth of local talent and ecosystems is considered paramount to advancing AI solutions that are of public value and interest, serving the Continent’s needs and priorities, and respecting and preserving cultural values and customs.
