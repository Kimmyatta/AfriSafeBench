Risk assessment and mitigation are necessary in the design and development of technologies for use in human health. Risk should be assessed at each stage of development and reassessed regularly with stakeholders. The aim of developers should be for the AI technology to achieve the intended outcomes with a reduced level of risk. All major trade-offs should be clearly identified and considered.
