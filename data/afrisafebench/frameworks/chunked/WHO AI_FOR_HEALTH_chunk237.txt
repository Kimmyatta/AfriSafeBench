ETHICAL CHALLENGES TO USE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE FOR HEALTH CARE 90% of all jobs in the United Kingdom’s NHS will require digital skills, including navigating the “data-rich” health-care environment, and also digital and genomics literacy. The requirement for digital literacy will not be limited to clinical care (although this section concentrates on clinical staff) but extends to health-care workers in public health, surveillance, the environment, prevention, protection, education, awareness, diet, nutrition and all the other social determinants of health that can be supported by AI.
