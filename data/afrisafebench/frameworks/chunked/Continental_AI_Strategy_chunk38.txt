The 44th Ordinary Session of the African Union Executive Council endorsed the Conceptual Framework on AI and tasked the AU Commission with expediting the development of a comprehensive, forward-looking, and action-oriented Continental AI Strategy to effectively harness AI's potential to transform the African economy and society in line with the AU Agenda 2063 goals while managing associated risks and harms. Against this background and building on the Conceptual Framework and outcome of the virtual multi-stakeholder consultations held in April 2024 on 4 topics, namely: (i) Maximising the Benefit of AI for Africa; (ii) Addressing and Mitigating AI Risks and Fostering the Ethical Governance of AI in Africa; (iii) Building Capabilities for AI in Africa, and (iv) Fostering Regional and International Cooperation on AI, the AU Commission, developed a Continental AI Strategy that reflects the aspirations of African people and stakeholders.
