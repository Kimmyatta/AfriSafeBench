ELEMENTS OF A FRAMEWORK FOR GOVERNANCE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE FOR HEALTH Assessing whether AI is necessary and appropriate for use in the public sector As for any use of AI by health professionals, governments must assess whether an AI technology is necessary and appropriate for the intended use and can be used according to its laws. The assessment could include an evaluation of whether use of AI is appropriate. In India, the Government’s internal think tank, Niti Aayog, has proposed constitution of an ethics committee to review procurement of AI in the public sector. According to a draft proposal released in 2020, the committee “may be constituted for the procurement, development, operations phase of AI systems and be made accountable for adherence to the Responsible AI principles” (322).
