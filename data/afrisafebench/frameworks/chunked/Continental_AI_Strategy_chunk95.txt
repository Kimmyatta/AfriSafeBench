Third, there is a need to establish a triple helix between research and civil society as they present the voices of the ordinary citizens, the private sector and the public sector in the design and implementation of AI solutions for public services in Africa. High-Level Recommendations o Promote the use of AI in the public sector by collecting information, sharing current AI use cases in governments and conducting research and analysis. o Promote the capacity of civil servants and decision-makers on the implementation and management of AI solutions; o Forge partnerships between the public and private sectors and research institutions on AI innovations. Associated Actions o Build capacity of AU Member States on how to leverage AI in the public sector, o Create innovation-friendly procurement processes across Africa. o Collaborate with development partners and the private sector to implement AI for public service delivery use cases in selected countries and roll out across the continent.
