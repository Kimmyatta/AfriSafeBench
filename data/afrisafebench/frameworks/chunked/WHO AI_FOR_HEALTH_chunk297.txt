LIABILITY REGIMES FOR ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE FOR HEALTH The design, quality assurance and deployment of AI technologies may involve many people, which could also complicate assignment of liability. Product liability could ensure that developers take all possible steps during development of an algorithm to reduce the likelihood of error, including using diverse, complete data sets to train the algorithm and improving the explainability of the software (259).
