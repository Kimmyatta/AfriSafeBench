However, AI will have a greater impact in some sectors than in others. Among the most promising are agriculture and food security, health, education, environmental protection, climate change mitigation, adaptation and building resilience. These priority sectors are also in line with Agenda 2063 and the SDGs. Africa is based on an agrarian economy. Therefore, agriculture and food security are important sectors for all countries on the continent.
