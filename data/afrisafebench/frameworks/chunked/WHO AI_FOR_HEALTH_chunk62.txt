Emerging trends in the use of AI in clinical care Several important changes imposed by the use of AI in clinical care extend beyond the provider–patient relationship. Four trends described here are: the evolving role of the patient in clinical care; the shift from hospital to home-based care; use of AI to provide “clinical” care outside the formal health system; and use of AI for resource allocation and prioritization. Each of these trends has ethical implications, as discussed below.
