ETHICAL CHALLENGES TO USE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE FOR HEALTH CARE Several ethical challenges are emerging with the use of AI for health, many of which are especially relevant to LMIC. These challenges must be addressed if AI technologies are to support achievement of universal health coverage. Use of AI to extend health-care coverage and services in marginalized communities in HIC can raise similar ethical concerns, including an enduring digital divide, lack of good-quality data, collection of data that incorporate clinical biases (as well as inappropriate data collection practices) and lack of treatment options after diagnosis.
