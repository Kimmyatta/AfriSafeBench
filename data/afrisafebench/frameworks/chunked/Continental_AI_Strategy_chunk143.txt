• Opportunities for lifelong learning: People’s participation and critical engagement in the sustainable development process are contingent on their having the necessary competencies and tools. Media and information literacy for all peoples in Africa is needed to show users how to use technology wisely and the benefits of AI for social and lifelong learning if deployed transparently. High-Level Recommendations 50 o Encourage the development of national Media and Information Literacy Policies of AU Member States, o Promote, implement and monitor model resources on Media and Information Literacy in line with international standards. o Raise awareness of AI implications on the integrity of information and stimulate the integration of Media and Information Literacy in primary, secondary, and tertiary education (including teacher education) as well as outside of schools, such as in digital spaces.
