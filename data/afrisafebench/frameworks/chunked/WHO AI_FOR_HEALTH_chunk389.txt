Greater public participation by a wide range of stakeholders is necessary to ensure that decisions about the introduction of an AI system in health care and elsewhere are not taken only by civil servants and companies but are based on public participation of a wider range of stakeholders, including representatives of public interest groups and leaders of vulnerable groups that are often not involved in making such decisions. Their perspectives should be obtained before and not only after identification of an adverse effect, which is too late. Appropriate collection, stewardship and use of data The collection, storage and use of data according to ethical and legal standards also applies to governments.
