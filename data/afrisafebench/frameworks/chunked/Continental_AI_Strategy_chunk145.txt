To address the ethical, legal and societal implications of AI, safeguards, awareness raising and agile and comprehensive AI governance systems and regulations are needed. For Africa to mitigate and manage the risks of AI on its economy and society, and ensure that AI respects human rights and dignity, inclusion, culture and values, safety, security, and environmental and ecosystem sustainability, government institutions, civil society, academia and industry should adopt a collaborative and balanced approach between innovation and security to promote AI for good and social benefits effectively.
