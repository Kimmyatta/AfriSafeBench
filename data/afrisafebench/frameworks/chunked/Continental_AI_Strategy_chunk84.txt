Development and roll-out of AI assessment and evaluation tools and institutional mechanisms: Recent research has highlighted the importance of independent review mechanisms in mitigating the risk of harm associated with AI research and the rollout of AI systems. In particular, impact assessments, including UNESCO’s Ethical Impact Assessment, have been developed as best practices in the field for evaluating and measuring the real and potential impact of AI systems on individuals and societies. These tools are important instruments for understanding and redressing the actual impact of an AI system in different societal contexts and can draw on a range of methodologies to generate evidence of impact, including consultation with affected communities. Continuous research and evaluation- Research is a crucial pillar of emerging governance frameworks.
