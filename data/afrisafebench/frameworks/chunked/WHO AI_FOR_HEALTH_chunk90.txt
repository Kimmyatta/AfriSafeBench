The uses described above are therefore not yet established. 3.5 The future of artificial intelligence for health While AI may not replace clinical decision-making, it could improve decisions made by clinicians. In settings with limited resources, AI could be used to conduct screening and evaluation if insufficient medical expertise is available, a common challenge in many resource-poor settings. Yet, whether AI can advance beyond narrow tasks depends on numerous factors beyond the state of AI science and on the trust of providers, patients and health-care professionals in AI-based technologies.
