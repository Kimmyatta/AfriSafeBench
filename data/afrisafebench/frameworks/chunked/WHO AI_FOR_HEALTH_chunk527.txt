Patient agency with regard to predictive algorithms Use of AI predictive analytics in health care raises ethical concern with respect to informed consent and individual autonomy in decisions about patient and consumer health. Specific considerations • The need for an AI technology should be assessed, with the risk of the technology to patient autonomy and well-being. • Patients should be allowed to refuse AI technologies for health.
