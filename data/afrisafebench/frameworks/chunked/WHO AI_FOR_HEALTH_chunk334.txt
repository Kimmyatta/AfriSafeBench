ELEMENTS OF A FRAMEWORK FOR GOVERNANCE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE FOR HEALTH Data hubs Numerous data hubs pool various types of health data for use by third parties, which depend on the type of data hub. Several government-sponsored data hubs have emerged. In the USA, two such hubs are the Precision Medicine Initiative (All of Us) (286) and the Department of Veteran Affairs health data hub. The EU is establishing a European Health Data Space to facilitate the exchange and sharing of health data (e.g. health records, genomics, registries) for purposes such as the delivery of primary care and the development of new treatments, medicines, medical devices and services, while ensuring that people have control of their own health data (287).
