Implementation of AI Strategy 3.1 Building Capacity The Continental AI Strategy underscores the importance of building capacity at all levels to release the benefits of AI. It places emphasis on the AU and its Implementing Agencies and Specialized Institutions, along with RECs, Regional Organizations and Development Partners, to play a central role in AI development, deployment and adoption across Africa.
