Further, as part of the implementation, the AU will facilitate coordination between AU Member States and international engagement with global partners on AI including mobilising financial and technical resources for priority AI projects., High-level Recommendations: o Establish mechanisms and institutions within the African Union, or empower existing ones, to build capacity and render technical assistance to AU Member States to domesticate this Continental AI Strategy and accelerate its implementation. o Build capacity within the institutions at the African Union to mainstream AI technologies and solutions in key sectors.
