The AU Strategy on Artificial Intelligence (AI) was adopted by the AU Executive Council during its 45th Ordinary Session held on to 18th – 19th July 2024, in Accra, The Republic of Ghana. 2 Executive Summary Artificial intelligence (AI) has great potential for Africa’s socio-economic transformation and cultural renaissance. It will contribute to the attainment of the African Union Agenda 2063 and Sustainable Development Goals.
