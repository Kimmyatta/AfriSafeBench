These ethical principles are intended to provide guidance to stakeholders about how basic moral requirements should direct or constrain their decisions and actions in the specific context of developing, deploying and assessing the performance of AI technologies for health. These principles are also intended to emphasize issues that arise from the use of a technology that could alter relations of moral significance.
