BUILDING AN ETHICAL APPROACH TO USE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE FOR HEALTH This section addresses how measures other than law and policy can ensure that AI improves human health and well-being. 7.1 Ethical, transparent design of technologies Although technology designers and developers play critical roles in designing AI tools for use in health, there are no procedures for credentialing or licensing such as those required for health-care workers. In the absence of formal qualifications for ethics in the AI field, it is not enough merely to call for personal adherence to values such as reproducibility, transparency, fairness and human dignity.
