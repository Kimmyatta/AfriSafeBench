To implement these principles and human rights obligations into practice, all stakeholders, whether designers and programmers, providers, and patients, as well as Ministries of Health and Ministries of Information Technology, must work together to integrate ethical norms at every stage of a technology’s design, development, and deployment. Finally, I would like to thank all experts, stakeholders, and partners in the UN family and beyond who made essential contributions to the development of this document. I hope that this report will help to ensure that the development and use of AI for health will be guided by appropriate ethical norms and standards, so all populations can equally benefit from the great promise of these technologies in the future.
