While it is often possible to explain to a patient why a specific treatment is the best option for a specific condition, it is not always possible to explain how that treatment works or its mechanism of action, because some medical interventions are used before their mode of action is understood (165). It may be more important to explain how a system has been validated and whether a particular use falls within the parameters with which the system can be expected to produce reliable results rather than explaining how an AI model arrives at a particular judgement (166).
