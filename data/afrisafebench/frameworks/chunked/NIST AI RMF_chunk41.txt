Robustness requires not only that the system perform exactly as it does under expected uses, but also that it should perform in ways that minimize potential harms to people if it is operating in an unexpectedsetting. Validity and reliability for deployed AI systems are often assessed by ongoing testing or monitoring that confirms a system is performing as intended.
