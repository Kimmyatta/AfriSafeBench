A requirement that both ministries of health and public and private health-care providers observe legal and ethical standards in the procurement of AI can encourage appropriate design of AI technologies and provide a safeguard against harm. The Government of the United Kingdom has established an analytical framework for use of AI (323), which consists of the following: whether the available data contain the required information; if it is ethical and safe to use the data and consistent with the Government’s data ethics framework; if there are sufficient data for training AI; whether the task is too large or repetitive for a human to undertake without difficulty; and whether AI will provide information that a team could use to achieve real-world outcomes.
