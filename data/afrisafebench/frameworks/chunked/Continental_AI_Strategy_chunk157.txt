RECs can play a key role in encouraging the public and private sectors to increase financing for AI in their respective regions. 2.4.6 Regional and International Cooperation on AI There are important rationales for regional and global cooperation in AI development and regulation. Collaboration and coordination among AU Member States, as well as international dialogues and partnerships, are important for sharing experiences, resources and capabilities in the field of AI.
