Governance of AI through regulatory frameworks and the ethical principles that should be considered are discussed in section 9.5. 22 ETHICS AND GOVERNANCE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE FOR HEALTH 5. KEY ETHICAL PRINCIPLES FOR USE OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE FOR HEALTH Ethical principles for the application of AI for health and other domains are intended to guide developers, users and regulators in improving and overseeing the design and use of such technologies. Human dignity and the inherent worth of humans are the central values upon which all other ethical principles rest.
