The AI field is one of the most researched and innovative areas of our time. Research on learning and reasoning, perception, representation, speech, and language modelling, as well as investigations on responsible AI and AI safety, is being conducted worldwide in different labs funded by the private and public sectors. Africa invests in AI research insignificantly, and it does not show up in citations in leading AI journals or in patents. The United States, China, India and Europe are global leaders in this area.
