A recent comparison of deep-learning algorithms with health-care professionals in detection of diseases by medical imaging showed that AI is equivalent to human medical judgement in specific domains and applications in specific contexts but also that “few studies present externally validated results or compare the performance of deep learning models and health-care professionals using the same sample” (14).
