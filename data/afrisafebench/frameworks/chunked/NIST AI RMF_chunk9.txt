Sustainability refers to the “state of the global system, including environmental, social, and economic aspects, in which the needs of the present are met without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs” (ISO/IEC TR 24368:2022). Responsible AI is meant to result in technology that is also equitable and accountable.
